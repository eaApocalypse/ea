Development Page

These lists and scenarios are still works in progress. Each list will be marked with an level of development as followed.
Draft: List is in progress of being writen up.
Experimental: New list ready for gaming, had none or limited games.
Developmental: Had a some play testing without any trouble.
Pre-Approved: Had a decent ammount of play testing and ready for games at a balanced level. But need games from more groups.
Approved: Approved and agreed balanced by the wider community.
